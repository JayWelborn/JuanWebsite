About Me

I was born in 1935 in Puerto Rico. I came to New York in the 1940s, and performed with Charlie Parker, Miles Davis, and John Coltraine before I came to my senses and joined the Army. In the Army I've done a thousand and seventeen awesome things. I'm actually amazingly cool.
